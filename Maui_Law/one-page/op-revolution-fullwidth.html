Personal Injury

The law firm of Hevizy Mallios has over 46 years combined experience in the area of personal injury litigation. We have secured significant jury verdicts and sizeable out-of-court settlements for individuals and their families involving truck and transportation accidents, medical negligence and dangerous premises. An unexpected injury can drastically impact your health, your job, your family and your future. When that injury results from someone else's negligence, you deserve compensation. We will fight for your rights, go toe-to-toe with insurance companies and win you fair and full compensation to help pay for your recovery.

When a visitor from another state or country is injured, questions about where and how to file a claim against the negligent person or company arise. Hevizy Mallios successfully maneuvers through the complex jurisdictional issues associated with filing a claim for clients who reside outside of Hawaii. No matter where you live, we are able to expeditiously litigate your case.
